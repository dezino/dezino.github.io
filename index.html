About

DEZINO is a student collective comprised of over a dozen architectural, landscape, and industrial designers. We utilize our variety of experiences and insights to generate design solutions that are user-centered, environmentally considerate, and universally accessible.

DEZINO maintains a non-hierarchical internal structure; our strength comes from the ability of all members to contribute leadership and input in varying capacities from project to project.

Japantown Peace Plaza Competition Project

Dezino members reimagined San Francisco's Japantown Peace Plaza for contemporary use. This project was designed as an entry to the Thomas Church Landscape Architecture Competition.

Woven Tower

This structure was fabricated as a celebration of curved form, completely assembled with string and hardware connections. The entire form can be dissassembled and reassembled anywhere.

Hyperbolic Paraboloid

Dezino members designed and formed a hyperbolic paraboloid form out of concrete. The project was an exploration of the limits of concrete form in both shape and color.
